Invariance

The generative artworks use the volume raymarching algorithm for the creation of cubes in a 3D scene. The artwork complexity results from a fine-tuning between cubes size, gutters size and the number of cubes along the three dimensions — as well as the application of set operations like union, intersection and complement.

Later in life, Albert Einstein said that he should have called his work invariant theory instead of relativity theory, to avoid the common misconception that the relativity theory provides a valid basis for the ambiguous assertion that Truth is relative. Of course, relativity theory has nothing to do with that, as much as this generative project has nothing to do with its name.
