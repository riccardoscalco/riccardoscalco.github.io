Slo Fuzz

Generative artworks based on a recursive application of space tiling functions. The result is a complex variety of perfectly aligned grids at different scales. Tiles dimensions, recursion depth, filling patterns and border size are all variables that shape the artwork appearance.

The project takes the name from the song Slo Fuzz by Sol Seppy. The song appears on the album The Bells of 1 2 — Sol Seppy wrote, sang, played and produced all 12 tracks on the album herself.
